# Station Accessibility Guide

## Accessible Entrances

Gate D (Level F3, Concourse) is the primary accessible entrance equipped with a ramp and level access. No steps are required at this entrance.

Gates A, B, and C are located on Level F4 (Transport Hub) and require use of elevators or escalators to reach the concourse and platform levels. For wheelchair users, elevator C1 connects F4 to F3.

## Elevators

- **Elevator C1**: Connects Level F4 (Transport Hub) to Level F3 (Concourse). Located near Gate B. Capacity: 13 persons / 1000 kg.
- **Elevator C2**: Connects Level F3 (Concourse) to Level F1 (Platform). Located at the center of the concourse. Capacity: 13 persons / 1000 kg.
- For full wheelchair-accessible route: Enter via Gate D → use Elevator C2 → Platform.

## Tactile Paths (Blind Paths)

Tactile guide tiles run continuously from Gate D → fare gates → Elevator C2 → Platform doors. All nodes on this path have `blind_path=True` in the navigation graph.

To request a tactile or accessible route, say: "Accessible route from entrance D to platform" or use the ♿ button in the Quick Actions panel.

## Fare Gates

Accessible-width fare gates (wider turnstiles) are available at positions FG-A3, FG-D1, FG-E1. These accommodate wheelchairs and strollers.

## Priority Seating

Priority seating for elderly, pregnant, and disabled passengers is available at both ends of each platform car. Signs are posted in blue.

## Assistance

A station staff desk is located at Level F3 near Gate D. Staff are available during all operating hours (05:30 – 23:30) to assist passengers with special needs.

For emergency assistance on the platform, press the SOS button on any platform door or use the intercom on the elevator.

## Strollers and Bicycles

Folded strollers and non-motorised bicycles may be brought through accessible fare gates. Please fold bicycles before entering.
