# Navigation Tips and Route Recommendations

## Recommended Routes by Use Case

### Standard Commuter (Peak Hours 07:30–09:00 and 17:30–19:30)

- **Best entrance**: Gate D (Level F3) — direct access to concourse, bypasses crowded F4 zone.
- **Route**: Enter Gate D → fare gate (south bank FG-S) → Escalator ESC-04 (up side, going down to platform) → Platform.
- **Avoid**: Gate A/B/C during morning peak — the F4 transport hub becomes extremely congested.

### Wheelchair / Accessible Route

- **Best entrance**: Gate D (F3, ground level with ramp).
- **Route**: Gate D → accessible fare gate FG-D1 → Elevator C2 → Platform.
- **Travel time**: approximately 4–6 minutes from Gate D to platform.

### Passengers with Large Luggage

- Use elevators (C1 if entering from F4, C2 for F3 to Platform).
- Luggage storage lockers near Gate B (F4) if you need to store bags before travelling.
- Wide fare gates FG-N11 or FG-N12 accommodate oversized bags.

### Fastest Route (Any Gate to Platform)

| From | Fastest connector | Approx. time |
|---|---|---|
| Gate A (F4) | ESC-01 down to F3 → ESC-04 down | ~4 min |
| Gate B (F4) | ESC-02 down to F3 → ESC-03 down | ~4 min |
| Gate C (F4) | ESC-05 down to F3 → ESC-04 down | ~5 min |
| Gate D (F3) | Walk to ESC-04 | ~2.5 min |

### Exit (Platform to Street)

For outbound passengers (platform → street), the recommended exit depends on your destination:
- East exit: Gates A or B (F4) — depart directly to the east plaza.
- West exit: Gate D (F3) — step out to the east plaza bus interchange.
- Gate C is recommended for connections to the long-distance bus terminal.

## Avoiding Congestion

Based on historical crowd data embedded in the navigation graph:
- **Most congested area**: Concourse (F3) fare gate north bank during peak hours.
- **Recommended bypass**: Use south bank fare gates (FG-S) and Escalator ESC-04.
- **Escalator ESC-03** (concourse north, down to platform) is the most heavily used escalator. During peak times, consider using Stair S1 (north end) for faster descent.

## Transfer Information

This station does not have a direct in-station transfer to another metro line. Buses connecting to other lines stop at Gate C (F4 east plaza, Lines 23 and 45) and Gate D (F3 west plaza, Lines 12 and 67).
