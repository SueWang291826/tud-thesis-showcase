# Station FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

## Operating Hours

- **First train**: 05:30 (weekdays and weekends)
- **Last train**: 23:30 (weekdays), 00:00 midnight (Friday and Saturday nights)
- **Station gates open**: 05:15 (30 minutes access before first service)
- **Station gates close**: 00:15 (15 minutes after last service)

## Ticketing and Fare

- Single-journey tickets are available at vending machines on Level F4 (all gates) and Level F3.
- Transit cards (Smart Card) can be topped up at all Level F3 service counters and vending machines.
- Airport Express and intercity transfers: purchase at Gate C (F4) service counter.
- Fares range from ¥3 (2 stops) to ¥14 (full line). Monthly passes available at service counter.

## WiFi

Free public WiFi (SSID: **MetroFreeWifi**) is available throughout the station on all levels. No registration required. Speed: up to 20 Mbps.

## Toilets / Restrooms

- **Level F3 (Concourse)**: Toilets located in the east wing between Gates D and E, and in the west wing near Gate C escalators. Both are accessible (wheelchair-friendly cubicles available).
- **Level F1 (Platform)**: Emergency toilets at the northern end of the platform (exit 1 side).
- Facilities include baby changing rooms and nursing rooms in the Level F3 west toilet block.

## Lost and Found

Lost property office is located on Level F3 near Gate D. Open 08:00–20:00 daily. After hours, report to the 24-hour security desk at Gate A (F4). Hotline: 400-123-4567.

## Luggage Storage

Coin-operated locker banks are located near Gate B (F4) and Gate D (F3). Sizes: small (¥5/4h), medium (¥10/4h), large (¥15/4h). Maximum 24-hour storage.

## Customer Service Counter

Located on Level F3 near Gate D. Open 07:00–21:00. Services: ticketing assistance, lost & found, accessibility support, complaints and feedback.

## Peak Hours

Weekday peak hours: **07:30–09:00** (morning) and **17:30–19:30** (evening). During peak hours, trains run every 3 minutes. Off-peak: every 5–8 minutes.

## Pets

Small pets in carriers (max 45×30×25 cm) are permitted during off-peak hours (09:30–16:30 and after 20:00) upon showing a valid rabies vaccination certificate. Large dogs are not permitted.

## Smoking

Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout the entire station and on all platforms. Designated smoking areas are outside all ground-level exits.
