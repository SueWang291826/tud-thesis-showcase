# Connector Reference — Station Vertical Connectors

## Overview

The station has three publicly accessible levels connected by a network of escalators, elevators, and stairs.

| Level | Name | Elevation |
|---|---|---|
| F1 | Platform (站台层) | 0 m |
| F3 | Concourse (站厅层) | 12.1 m |
| F4 | Transport Hub (交通层) | 17.4 m |

Note: Level F2 (Equipment, 5.3 m) is non-public and accessible only to maintenance staff.

## Escalators

| ID | Route | Direction | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| ESC-01 | F4 ↔ F3 | Both (two separate banks) | East corridor near Gate A |
| ESC-02 | F4 ↔ F3 | Both | West corridor near Gate B |
| ESC-03 | F3 ↔ F1 | Down only | Concourse center-north |
| ESC-04 | F3 ↔ F1 | Up only | Concourse center-south |
| ESC-05 | F4 ↔ F3 | Both | Gate C entry corridor |

Escalators run continuously from station opening (05:15) to closing (00:15). In the event of a fault, escalators may be switched to stair mode (manual walking only).

## Elevators

| ID | Route | Capacity | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| ELV-C1 | F4 ↔ F3 | 13 persons / 1000 kg | Operational |
| ELV-C2 | F3 ↔ F1 | 13 persons / 1000 kg | Operational |

Both elevators are wheelchair accessible (door width: 90 cm). Priority use for elderly, disabled, and passengers with luggage. Intercom button inside each elevator connects directly to the station control room.

## Fare Gates

Fare gate banks are located on Level F3 in three clusters:

- **North bank (FG-N)**: 12 gates including 2 wide-access gates (FG-N11, FG-N12)
- **South bank (FG-S)**: 10 gates including 2 wide-access gates (FG-S9, FG-S10)
- **Service gate (FG-SVC)**: Adjacent to Customer Service Counter for staff and accessibility

Fare gates accept transit cards, QR code tickets, and NFC mobile payments.

## Platform Screen Doors (PSD)

192 platform screen door nodes are mapped on Level F1. Doors open automatically when trains arrive. Emergency release handles are located on the trackside of every door. In a train evacuation, staff will manually open specific doors.
